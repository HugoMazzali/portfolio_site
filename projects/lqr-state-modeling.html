Balancing Beam

Utilizing Matlab and LQR System Dynamics modeling, I designed a simulation for a beam to balance a ball

Overview

As a systems engineering project, myself and two of my friends wrote a MatLab script that uses LQR state-space modeling to simulate a beam balancing a ball with an initial impulse. We wrote a short paper explaining our program and mathematical model development. You can find a link to the PDF version of this paper below.
